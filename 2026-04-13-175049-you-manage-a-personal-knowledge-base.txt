   - report/whitepaper: section-by-section

become one-sided.

two sentences. This is used for index scanning.                                 
                                                                                
5. When a query produces a valuable answer (comparison, analysis, connection),  
save it as a new wiki page with `type: query-result` in frontmatter. Queries    
compound the wiki, not just ingests.                                            
                                                                                
6. Every wiki page has frontmatter:                                             
   ```yaml                                                                      
   ---                                                                          
   title:                                                                       
   type: summary | entity | concept | comparison | query-result                 
   sources: []                                                                  
   created: YYYY-MM-DD                                                          
   updated: YYYY-MM-DD                                                          
   tags: []                                                                     
   ---                                                                          
   ```                                                                          
                                                                                
7. Run a lint pass after every 5-10 ingests. Fix what you can autonomously.     
Flag anything that needs user input."

1. separate your vaults                                                         
                                                                                
the obsidian founder said this. dont let agent-generated content mix with your  
personal notes.                                                                 
                                                                                
> clean vault for your own thinking                                             
> separate vault for your agents                                                
> your personal notes stay high signal                                          
2. classify before you extract                                                  
                                                                                
dont treat every document the same.                                             
                                                                                
> classify by type first (report vs transcript vs article vs thread)            
> run type-specific extraction for each                                         
> a 50-page report needs different handling than a 2-page letter                
> set token budgets so you dont burn context on low-value sources               
3. add a bias check                                                             
                                                                                
if you ingest 5 articles praising the same framework, the LLM will confidently  
confirm your bias.                                                              
                                                                                
every time the LLM updates a concept page, force it to generate a               
"counter-arguments and data gaps" section.                                      
                                                                                
makes it look for the strongest critique of whatever its summarizing            
4. add a TLDR to every wiki page                                                
                                                                                
> LLM scans the index, reads the TLDR, decides whether to read the full page    
> saves tokens on pages that arent relevant to the current query                
> you skim your own wiki faster too                                             
5. file your queries back into the wiki                                         
                                                                                
when you ask a question and get a good answer, that answer becomes a new wiki   
page. comparisons, analyses, connections you found.                             
                                                                                
skip this and your best thinking disappears into chat history                   
6. plan for scale early                                                         
                                                                                
two things break first:                                                         
                                                                                
> past a few hundred pages, the index file isnt enough. you need real search    
> structure creeps in whether you plan for it or not. frontmatter, naming       
conventions, folder rules                                                       
                                                                                
set these up on day 1                                                           
7. run lint passes                                                              
                                                                                
periodically ask your LLM to audit the wiki:                                    
                                                                                
> find contradictions between pages                                             
> flag claims that newer sources have replaced                                  
> spot orphan pages with no inbound links                                       
> suggest concepts mentioned but missing their own page

  Purpose: Audit và update content của một wiki page cụ thể. Khác với wiki:audit
   (structural) — đây là content refresh.

  Workflow:

  1. Read target page (e.g., wiki/rowboat.md)
  2. Parse frontmatter — identify sources:, updated:, type:
  3. Content audit:
    - Claims still accurate? (cross-check with other wiki pages)
    - Sources still accessible? (validate URLs in raw/)
    - Counter-arguments section up-to-date?
    - Related pages still exist? (check [[wikilinks]])

  5. Optional fresh research: if user passes --research flag, do web search for
  updates to the topic
  6. Diff proposal: show user what would change — don't auto-apply
  7. On approval: update page, bump updated:, add ## [YYYY-MM-DD] refresh entry
  to wiki/log.md
  8. Preserve: original content in page history (git handles this), never
  silently delete

  Invocation:
  /wiki:refresh wiki/rowboat.md                    # audit only
  /wiki:refresh wiki/rowboat.md --research         # + fresh web research
  /wiki:refresh wiki/rowboat.md --all-sources      # re-read all sources in

  ❌ Ý tưởng KHÔNG nên adopt

  1. "Map of a mind" framing
  - Không apply. Vault của bạn không phải about one person's life. Research wiki
   + project knowledge có aims khác.

  2. 30+ pre-defined directory taxonomy
  - philosophies/, patterns/, tensions/, identities/, touchstones/,
  metaphors/... — journal-specific. Bạn đã deliberately kept flat. Giữ nguyên.

  3. ingest.py Python script dependency
  - Bạn drop files manually vào raw/. Không cần format parser cho Day
  One/iMessage/Apple Notes. Skip entire ingest command.

  4. Chronological absorb loop
  - Personal journal có chronology ý nghĩa. Research/code knowledge thì không.
  Ingest by source, not by date.

  5. Tone rules cứng (no em-dashes, no "legendary", no rhetorical questions)
  - Quá opinionated. Wikipedia tone là good default nhưng đừng ban em-dashes.
  Technical writing cần nuance này.

  6. Length targets per article type cứng
  - "Person (1 reference) 20-30 lines" — irrelevant cho technical wiki. Soft
  guideline OK, hard target nonsense.

  7. Single mega-skill với 6 commands (ingest, absorb, query, cleanup,
  breakdown, status)
  - Megaskill antipattern. Bạn đã decide tách thành 8 skills có prefix wiki:.
  Đúng rồi, giữ.

  ---
  🔴 Critiques nghiêm trọng của Farzaa's skill

  1. Zero counter-arguments discipline
  - Không có mandatory "counter-arguments and data gaps" section. Vì nó là
  personal journal → inherently one-sided. Nhưng với research wiki của bạn,
  counter-arguments là critical (Karpathy infographic rule #3).
  - Không được drop rule này.

  2. No uncertainty notation
  - Farzaa's skill không có [uncertainty: reason]. Mọi claim presented as fact.
  OK cho personal memory, nguy hiểm cho research.
  - Giữ [uncertainty:] notation từ wiki-knowledge-compiler.

  3. Weak provenance
  - Citations chỉ là entry IDs trong frontmatter. Không có inline source
  attribution cho từng claim. Research wiki cần stronger — inline citations khi
  claim controversial hoặc single-source.

  4. No search tier strategy
  - Assumption: grep đủ. Không scale. Bạn đã có tier strategy (grep → QMD CLI →
  QMD MCP). Better.

  5. "Rebuild index only at end" rule conflicts với incremental ingest
  - Nếu session crash, index out of sync. Bạn nên update index-on-ingest
  (already trong current workflow).
